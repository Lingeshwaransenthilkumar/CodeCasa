About the author

Christopher C. Doyle is an author who transports the reader into a fascinating world where ancient secrets buried in legends blend with science and history to create a gripping story. His debut novel, The Mahabharata Secret, featured among the top ten books of 2013 and was nominated for the Raymond Crossword Book Award, 2014. He followed it up with the bestselling The Mahabharata Quest series, featuring The Alexander Secret and The Secret of the Druids and the bestselling The Pataala Prophecy featuring Son of Bhrigu and The Mists of Brahma, all of which have won rave reviews from readers.
